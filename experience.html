My Journey at Wayfair

Role: Machine Learning Engineer • Duration: 2024 - Present

During my time at Wayfair, I've been at the forefront of revolutionizing how customers discover and shop for home furnishings. Leading a team of talented engineers, we've developed cutting-edge ML models that understand customer preferences and shopping patterns.

One of our key achievements was implementing a real-time recommendation system that increased customer engagement by 40%. The system uses advanced deep learning techniques to understand product relationships and user behavior patterns.

We also pioneered the use of computer vision algorithms to help customers visualize furniture in their homes before purchase, leading to a significant reduction in return rates and improved customer satisfaction.
